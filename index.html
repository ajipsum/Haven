Apply Now Become a Haven

We partner with home sharing services to provide homes to those looking to escape domestic violence.

Our service is 100% confidential to protect the anonymity of victims.

  • Fast and easy to sign up.
  • Your data is confidential and secure.
  • Matches you to participating home sharers to relocate you as soon as possible.

How It Works

Home sharing services such as Airbnb and HomeAway allow their users to donate their spaces to Haven when they have no bookings. We match victims who apply to our service with home sharers in their area to allow them to relocate safely and promptly. All applicants are kept confidential and no matches are disclosed.

Home Sharers Donate Their Spaces

Participating donors are available to match when the have free bookings available.

Applicants are Matched to a Home

We find a haven as soon as possible to allow victims to escape domestic abuse.

Victims are Relocated

We provide transport for victims to their haven. All matches and victim information is kept confidential.
